About

Born and raised in Miami, FL.

Blending science and art.

Emily Fakhoury is a visual artist based in South Florida. She blends art and science to produce mesmerizing chemigrams that add intrigue and distinction to residential and commercial spaces. With training in biochemistry and expertise in photography, Emily enjoys working with chemical processes that produce unique art pieces.

Artist Statement

The work of Emily Fakhoury is highly experimental and innovative. By using multiple chemicals on light sensitive media, mysteriously creative images appear that stir the imagination and inspiration of the viewer. Rooms featuring the art work take on the aura of freedom and uninhibited emotion. Colors, patterns, shading, and three dimensional motifs generate a cascade of motion. The works become windows to other worldly dimensions. Imagination is untethered and uncoreographed producing boundless new universes for your exploration.
